Specifications

  • Frame: Martian II RX250

  • HD Camera: GoPro Hero 5 session
  • VTx: Eachine TX805
  • Rx: FrSky XM+
  • GPS: Beitian BN-180
  • Buzzer: ViFly Finder 2

FPV video

This is the first in a series I'm doing on my journey into the FPV freestyle hobby, this video showcases the agility and flexibility of the quadcopters flight characteristics.

This quad is capable of various missions at about 10 minutes of flight time with a CNHL 1500mAh 4S 120C LiPo battery.

This quad has been programmed with betaflight to return to home using it's onboard GPS, emit an independantly powered distress beep/light & has a tuned video transmitter for better video link range and quality to my SJ-RG01 goggles.
